WYSIWYG In the WYSIWYG(What You See Is What You Get) editor,

Your edits are automatically saved.

The WYSIWYG screen shows the screen to type in your project details in coded format following the formatting instructions.

Clicking the Preview button displays your project how it would look when you have completed the import.

Clicking the File save button exports your formatted project detail in .txt so you can keep or share it with others.

Clicking the Complete button adds your current edit to the main projects list.
